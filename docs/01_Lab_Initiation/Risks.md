# Project Risks

## Potential Risks

1. **Technical Delay**: Development may take longer than expected due to unforeseen technical challenges.
2. **Budget Overrun**: Increased requirements may lead to exceeding the initial budget.
3. **Resource Availability**: Key team members may be unavailable during critical phases of the project.
4. **Scope Creep**: Additional features or requirements may be introduced, complicating development and timelines.
5. **Technology Risks**: The chosen technologies may not work as intended or have unanticipated issues.
6. **Stakeholder Changes**: Changes in stakeholder requirements or priorities can affect the project outcomes.

## Mitigation Strategies

- Regular project reviews to ensure timelines and budgets are on track.
- Contingency plans for resource allocation and budgeting.
- Clear communication to manage expectations and document any changes in scope.
- Continuous monitoring of technological choices and adjustments as necessary.
